About Me


I was born in Ulaanbaatar Mongolia and was raised in the United States since I was 7 years old. I am bilingual in both English and Mongolian. Prior to my career as a Realtor with Century 21 North Homes Realty, Inc I owned and managed a Licensed Family Childcare Business for 4 years as well as being a Certified Nursing Assistant. With other customer service experience from Nordstroms to Gene Juarez Salon and Spas, I have the passion and professionalism in my work ethics, especially with finding and selling your home.
